How to Play

  • Player 1: Use W (up) and S (down) keys to move your paddle.
  • Player 2: Use Up and Down arrow keys to move your paddle.
  • Try to bounce the ball back and prevent it from passing your paddle.
  • Score points by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle.
  • The first player to reach the winning score wins!
